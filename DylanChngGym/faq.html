Frequent Ask Question

Membership Information

Q: What is FitnessPass?

Answer

FitnessPass is a monthly fitness membership that provides you with access to different studios, gyms, and wellness offerings across ClassPass cities.

Q: How many times can I visit a studio each cycle ?

Answer

The number of classes you can take each cycle depends on your membership plan. You can book classes ranging from yoga to barre to boxing to martial arts – the options are endless!

Q: Can I see a list of studios available ?

Answer

To see a list of studios that ClassPass partners with: • Make sure you're not logged into a RoyalPass account (you'll be redirected otherwise) • Go to the RoyalPass homepage • Click on "Explore Studios" at the top of the screen
